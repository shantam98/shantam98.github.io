## üí° Pro Tips

- **Be Honest**: Only list skills and projects you can discuss confidently
- **Show Impact**: Use metrics and numbers wherever possible
- **Keep Updated**: Set calendar reminders to update your portfolio
- **Get Feedback**: Ask mentors or peers to review before sharing widely
- **A/B Test**: Try different headlines or project orders to see what works
- **Mobile First**: Most visitors will view on mobile, ensure it looks great
- **Fast Loading**: Optimize images and keep page lightweight
- **Clear CTA**: Make it easy for visitors to contact you
